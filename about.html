About Me Welcome to My World

Hi! My name is Maxim, I'm 18 years old and I live in Uzbekistan. At the moment I have been engaged in photography for more than 3 years and videography around 1 year. In my childhood, I became acquainted with the art of photography. It all began with a glance at the National Geographic magazine. Picturesque landscapes, expressive portraits and beautiful architecture only inspired me. When I got older, I decided to pick up a camera and create my own collection of such photos. Since then, I have been purposefully going to my goal, creating my own albums of various photographs.
